Who We Are

We are a privately owned website with children and siblings who are diagnosed with Autism Spectrum Disorder. Out of a desire to help other family members find the programs they need, we work towards giving resources to the community.

How We Research

All locations and programs that are reflected on this website are ones we have used, experienced, and can support to other family members. No programs will be added on unless thoroughly researched and vetted by our families.

How to Join

To comment on locations and to send recommendations to be added to the list, users must Sign up and verify that whether users have families or have extensive experience with working with individuals with ASD.

Our Mission Statement

Autism Spectrum Disorder is a mental disorder that changes not only the individuals' life afflicted by it, but also changes the life of those who are close to them. Despite the numerous programs and aid available within Georgia, as parents and family members, we can't help but worry whether the program we see are truly fit for our loved ones.
